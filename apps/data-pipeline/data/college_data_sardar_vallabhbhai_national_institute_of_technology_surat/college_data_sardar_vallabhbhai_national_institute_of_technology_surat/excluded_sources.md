# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source types were not used for verified numerical facts:

- **College Pravesh, Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and similar portals:** useful for discovery only; cutoff, fee, placement and seat figures were excluded unless traced to an official source.
- **Coaching/college-prediction websites:** predicted 2026 cutoffs and admission probabilities were excluded.
- **Copied placement tables and social-media graphics without an official report:** excluded because scope, year and units could not be verified.
- **Anonymous one-line reviews and rumours:** excluded from campus-reality synthesis when they lacked detail or independent support.
- **Personal accusations against identifiable individuals:** not retained.
- **Claims about a specific company's package, role or branch eligibility:** excluded unless the official CDC publication explicitly supported them.
- **Google-search/AI summaries:** not treated as sources.
- **Private WhatsApp/Telegram groups and inaccessible accounts:** not accessed.

Serious public allegations about mess hygiene, ragging, medical care or hostel administration are preserved only as carefully labelled student-reported evidence, with limitations and no assumption of college-wide truth.

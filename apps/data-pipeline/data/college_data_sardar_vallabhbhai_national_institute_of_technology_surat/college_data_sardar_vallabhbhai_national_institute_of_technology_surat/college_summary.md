## 1. Verified college overview - official verified
SVNIT Surat was established in 1961 as SVRCET, became a deemed university/NIT in 2002 and an Institute of National Importance in 2007. Its official JoSAA code is **229** and its main campus is at Ichchhanath, Surat. The official May 2026 RTI disclosure reports a 250-acre campus.

## 2. Accreditation and ranking - official verified / unavailable
NIRF Engineering ranks were **66 in 2025 (score 50.77)**, **59 in 2024 (51.04)** and **65 in 2023 (49.00)**. Current programme-wise NBA validity, NAAC status and a separate current AICTE institute ID require manual confirmation.

## 3. Admission routes - official verified
Regular UG/integrated admission is through JEE Main and JoSAA, with CSAB Special for vacancies and DASA/other foreign-national routes under their current rules. Home-state and other-state pools apply. Branch change is not permitted in the 2026 JoSAA profile.

## 4. Branches and intake - official verified
The 2026 JoSAA matrix contains **15 programmes and 1,400 seats**, with **zero female-supernumerary seats**: 11 B.Tech programmes, one integrated B.Tech.-M.Tech. AI programme and three integrated B.Sc.-M.Sc. programmes.

## 5. Historical cutoffs - official verified historical evidence
The package contains **4,767 numeric rows**: **4,346 JoSAA rows** across 2023-2025 and **421 CSAB 2025 rows**. Thirty-one 2024 JoSAA records with blank ranks were excluded rather than treated as zero. The 2024 `AI / All India` quota label remains flagged. CSAB PDFs state all ranks are All India CRL, so reserved CSAB rows must not be directly compared with JoSAA category ranks. These are not official 2026 cutoffs.

## 6. Fees and total cost - official verified plus calculated
For a standard full-fee B.Tech entrant, published tuition is ₹62,500 per semester. The calculated four-year institute cash flow including other published charges, refundable security and seat rent is ₹676,000. Adding the JoSAA-profile hostel approximation of ₹45,000 per year gives about **₹856,000**, excluding mess and personal costs. SC/ST/PwD students and eligible income groups receive the published tuition waivers/remissions.

## 7. Scholarships - official verified / verification required
Official material lists national merit/merit-cum-means and category schemes, Dr. A. P. Verma, Nishith Vijay Sampat and Rajanikumar Majumadar scholarships, plus fee remission. Government schemes may be potentially applicable, but current amounts, deadlines and sanction are not guaranteed.

## 8. Placement statistics - institution-published and NIRF-reported
Official placement summaries cover **2023-24, 2024-25 and provisional 2025-26**. The provisional 2025-26 overall B.Tech record is 493 placed out of 740 registered/eligible, median CTC 10.89 LPA, average 12.42 LPA and highest 62 LPA. The 2024-25 overall B.Tech record reports 592/720, median 10 LPA, average 11.83 LPA and highest 74 LPA. Preserve the NIRF 2023-24 placed count of 581 separately from the CDC count of 585 because scopes differ.

## 13. Location and travel - official plus map-derived
The campus is in Ichchhanath on Surat-Dumas Road, about 10 km from both Surat Railway Station and Surat International Airport according to the JoSAA profile. Travel times and coordinates are map-derived approximations.

## 14. Student-reported campus reality - student-reported, conflicting
Repeated themes suggest that coding opportunities are present but self-driven, academic workload varies by branch, and events/clubs are active. Hostel and mess experience varies sharply by building; Bhabha Bhavan has repeated recent negative reports. Reports about ragging, restrictions, sports and medical experience conflict and must not be generalized.

## 15. Parent Mode summary - perspective
The key strengths are national-institute status, urban connectivity, tuition remission, broad programmes and verified placement reports. The key checks are allotted hostel condition, complete four-year living cost, scholarship sanction and final placement data.

## 16. Student Mode summary - perspective
Choose by branch fit and curriculum, not only institute tag. For software goals outside CSE/AI, verify recruiter access and expect self-driven preparation. Use clubs, projects and internships early; inspect the actual hostel rather than relying on campus-wide claims.

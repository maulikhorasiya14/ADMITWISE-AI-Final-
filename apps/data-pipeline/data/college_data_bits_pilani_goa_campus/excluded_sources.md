# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Unofficial college-information aggregators

Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar aggregator pages were not used for verified cutoffs, fees, seat intake, accreditation or placements. Their tables can be useful discovery leads but were excluded because the prompt requires original official evidence.

## Search snippets and AI summaries

Search-engine answer boxes, generated summaries and copied tables without an identifiable primary source were excluded from structured factual records.

## Unsupported placement claims

Branch-wise package claims, “average package” graphics, social-media placement spreadsheets and anonymous package anecdotes were excluded. The official Goa placement PDF and official recruiter PDF are the only sources used for quantitative placement and recruiter records.

## Cutoff predictions

Predicted 2026 BITSAT cutoffs, coaching-site expected scores and historical-score extrapolations were excluded. The package contains completed official 2023–2025 final scores only.

## Seat-count estimates

No seat count was inferred from class size, cutoff movement, registration totals or student comments. Intake remains null until an official seat matrix is obtained.

## Hostel and mess rumours

One-line reviews, allegations involving identifiable individuals, private-group messages and incident rumours were excluded. Only short, non-identifying qualitative themes from public discussions were retained.

## Misleading campus mixing

University-wide NIRF and NAAC records are explicitly scoped as university-wide. Pilani- or Hyderabad-campus-specific placement data, hostel data and club lists were not imported as Goa-campus facts.

## Inaccessible/private material

Login-only groups, private Discord/WhatsApp/Telegram content, deleted posts, paywalled sources and pages requiring bypass of access controls were not accessed or reproduced.

# Research Log — BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa Campus

**Research date:** 2026-06-26  
**Target admission year:** 2026

## Searches performed

- Official Goa-campus identity, establishment, address and facilities
- BITSAT 2026 admissions, eligibility, programme matrix and FAQ
- Official historical BITSAT final cutoff scores
- 2026-27 fee schedule and scholarship provisions
- NIRF, NAAC and deemed-university status
- Goa-campus placement statistics and recruiter disclosures
- Official student clubs, centres, events and activity archives
- Public student discussions on hostels, grading, clubs, placements and campus life
- Public campus-tour videos with visual evidence
- Map-derived connectivity and hospital/transport estimates

## Official portals checked

- BITS Pilani university and Goa-campus websites
- BITS first-degree admission portal
- BITS scholarship and fee pages
- NIRF rankings
- Official Goa placement and recruiter PDFs

## Documents reviewed

- Goa Campus Placement Statistics for Three Years
- List of Recruiters for Academic Year 2023-24
- Official fee, admission, cutoff and scholarship webpages
- Official campus facilities, medical, library and activity pages

## Videos reviewed or catalogued

Three public campus/student-life videos were catalogued for visual qualitative evidence. Placement claims in videos were not used as verified figures.

## Reddit/forum discussions reviewed

Recent public discussions were reviewed for room allocation, grading, campus life, club induction, placement culture, branch eligibility and attendance. Usernames were not stored.

## Inaccessible or incomplete information

- No official branch-wise seat matrix found
- No verified current campus-specific AICTE ID or NBA validity table
- No final 2026 cutoff page yet
- No Goa-campus placement outcome table newer than 2022-23 found
- No public branch-wise placement table
- No current detailed hostel-capacity/allocation document
- Many club pages lack recent dated activity
- Some official pages expose only general university-wide data

## Extraction limitations

BITS publishes historical cutoff scores rather than ranks or category/quota/round combinations. Required cutoff dimensions were therefore filled with explicit “not differentiated” descriptors, and rank fields remain null.

Placement salary figures are total Goa-campus domestic values for FD+HD, not B.E.-branch-specific. Recruiter evidence confirms participation but not role, package, branch eligibility or selected count.

## Assumptions avoided

- No intake estimated from cutoffs or student counts
- No 2026 cutoff prediction labelled official
- No future hostel/mess inflation projected
- No company role or package inferred from its name
- No student opinion converted into official fact
- No absolute campus-safety claim
- No club marked active solely because it appears in an old archive

## Final status

The 18-file package is ready for human review. Admissions, branches, completed historical cutoffs, fees, location and older campus-specific placement records are structured. Seat intake, current accreditation details, latest placement outcomes, hostel operations and many club-activity records remain blocking or non-blocking manual-verification items.

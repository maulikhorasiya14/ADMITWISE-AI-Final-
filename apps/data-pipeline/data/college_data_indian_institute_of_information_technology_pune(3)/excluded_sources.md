# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source classes were encountered or considered but were **not used as verified factual evidence**.

## College Pravesh
- Encountered for historical cutoff tables.
- Excluded because it is an unofficial aggregator prohibited by the research specification.
- No 2024 cutoff value from it was entered into the verified dataset.

## Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and GetMyUni
- Encountered in search results for cutoffs, fees, reviews and placements.
- Excluded from verified facts because values may be copied, rounded, mixed across years or lack the original document.
- Their figures were not used to fill missing official records.

## Coaching/admission-prediction websites
- Excluded because predicted closing ranks and trend projections are not official cutoffs.
- No prediction is labelled as a 2026 cutoff.

## Search-engine/AI summaries
- Excluded as evidence because snippets may be stale or omit scope/year.
- Original official pages/documents were preferred.

## Undated recruiter-logo interpretations
- Official recruiter logos were retained only as “officially listed, year unavailable.”
- They were not treated as proof that a company currently visits, hires a particular branch or offers a stated package.

## Sensational or unsourced YouTube placement claims
- Titles such as “high fees / low placement” were treated only as prompts for verification.
- No package, percentage or recruiter claim from such videos entered the official placement tables.

## Anonymous one-line accusations and rumours
- Excluded from the public dataset because they cannot establish college-wide truth and may involve private individuals.

## Old transit-campus reviews generalized to 2026
- Historical reviews were retained only to explain campus-mixing risk.
- They were not used as current permanent-campus quality evidence.


## Superseded cutoff aids

- **Gurujada 2025 CSE/ECE reproductions (previously S021-S022):** removed from the active source register because the supplied merged dataset now provides every 2025 JoSAA round. They may be used only for a manual spot-check, never as authoritative evidence.
- **Zollege-hosted 2023 Round 4 reproduction (previously S023):** removed from active row provenance because the merged dataset supplies all 2023 JoSAA rounds.
- **Earlier pasted 2025 Round 6 table (previously S026):** superseded by the wider merged dataset. The values were not discarded, but their row-level provenance now resolves through S027, the original verified merged cutoff dataset.
- **Eight blank merged-dataset placeholders:** excluded from `cutoffs.csv` because both opening and closing ranks were empty. They represent no numeric cutoff and must not be converted to zero.

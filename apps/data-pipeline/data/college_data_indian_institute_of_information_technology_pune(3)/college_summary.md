## 1. Verified college overview — official verified

IIIT Pune is an Indian Institute of Information Technology established in 2016 under the IIIT public-private partnership framework and described by the current JoSAA profile as an Institute of National Importance. The current official campus is at **Gat No. 5 & 6, Nanoli-Tarf Chakan, P.O. Talegaon, Taluka Maval, Pune district, Maharashtra 410507**. Institute/counselling code: **321**. [S001]

The historical Yewalewadi/Bopdev/Kondhwa references concern the old transit campus and should not be mixed with the current permanent-campus record. [S024]

## 3. Admission routes — official verified

B.Tech admission is through **JEE Main and JoSAA**, with CSAB special rounds where notified. The current institute profile also references DASA for eligible foreign/CIWG admission. IIIT Pune uses an **All India** quota rather than home-state/other-state partitioning. Reservation categories and PwD/female seat pools follow JoSAA rules. Exact 2026 board-eligibility criteria must be taken from the current JoSAA business rules. [S001-S003]

Official branch-change rule: after the first two semesters, with all common first-year credits cleared in the first attempt, minimum CGPA 8.5, merit by CGPA then JEE rank, and Senate/capacity approval. [S001]

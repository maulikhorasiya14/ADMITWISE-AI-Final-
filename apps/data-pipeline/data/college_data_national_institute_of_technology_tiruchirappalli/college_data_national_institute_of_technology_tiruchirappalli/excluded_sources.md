# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source classes were encountered or anticipated but were excluded from verified factual fields:

- **Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and College Pravesh:** third-party aggregators; figures can be stale, transformed or copied without a primary-source audit trail.
- **Coaching/choice-filling sites and unsourced cutoff tables:** not used because the supplied JoSAA/CSAB material provides official-source data.
- **Search-engine or AI-generated summaries:** used only to locate original pages, never as evidence.
- **Reddit, YouTube, Medium and GeeksforGeeks campus narratives:** retained only in `student_experience_sources.csv` and `campus_reality.json`; not used for fees, cutoffs, placements, accreditation or intake.
- **Unofficial placement spreadsheets/screenshots and current-batch rumours:** excluded because denominator, eligibility and verification could not be established.
- **Old scholarship circular amounts and deadlines:** not copied into current benefit fields unless the 2026-27 call itself was available.
- **Unverified map distances, coordinates and cost-of-living estimates:** left null rather than guessed.
- **Public usernames and personal accusations:** not stored; sensitive claims were summarized without identifying individuals.

## 1. Verified college overview — Official verified

Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur (VNIT Nagpur), institute code **230**, is a Central Government National Institute of Technology and Institute of National Importance. The official profile identifies a 215-acre main campus at South Ambazari Road, Nagpur, Maharashtra 440010. It was established in 1960 and became an NIT/deemed autonomous institution in 2002. Sources: SRC001, SRC007, SRC008.

## 2. Accreditation and ranking — Official verified / unavailable where noted

- NIRF Engineering: **44 in 2025 (56.58)**, **39 in 2024 (57.89)** and **41 in 2023 (55.03)**.
- NIRF Architecture and Planning: **10 in 2025 (61.22)**.
- Institute of National Importance status is verified.
- Current NAAC grade, AICTE institute ID and programme-wise NBA validity remain **unavailable / manual verification required**.

## 3. Admission routes — Official verified

B.Tech. admission is through JEE Main and JoSAA; B.Arch. is through the applicable JEE Main Paper 2A/JoSAA route. The 2026 matrix uses Maharashtra and Other Than Maharashtra quotas with category and gender pools. CSAB Special and DASA/CIWG routes apply subject to current rules and vacancies. Branch change is listed as unavailable. Source: SRC001.

## 4. Branches and intake — Official verified

The 2026 JoSAA matrix contains **14 programmes** and **1,143 seats**, with zero female-supernumerary seats. The package preserves category-wise and gender-wise quota records separately in `college_profile.json`. New programmes should not be assumed to have historical placement trends.

## 6. Fees and total cost — Official verified + calculated

The accessible 2026 profile shows standard semester academic charges of INR 67,725 and a one-time charge of INR 15,500 for B.Tech. / INR 16,800 for B.Arch. Standard tuition, SC/ST/PwD waiver and income-based remissions are structured in `fees.csv`. Calculated totals exclude hostel, mess and personal costs and are explicitly labelled as estimates. The detailed current residential fee is unavailable.

## 7. Scholarships — Official verified / potentially applicable

Verified assistance includes SC/ST/PwD tuition waiver, full tuition remission below INR 1 lakh family income, and two-thirds remission for the INR 1–5 lakh band, subject to current documents and approval. NSP and MahaDBT schemes are listed only as potentially applicable. No scholarship is guaranteed.

- The final residential cost cannot yet be calculated reliably. — Current hostel and mess fee details were not fully accessible.
- The 2024-25 figure of 692 is job offers, not confirmed unique students placed. — Institution profile wording differs from NIRF placement-cohort metrics.
- Newer programmes have limited historical cutoff and placement evidence. — Several 2026 programmes do not appear in the 2023-25 historical branch set.
- Scholarship eligibility and deadlines may change annually. — Only broad fee-remission rules and government portals are verified; awards are never guaranteed.
- Current club activity and hostel rules may differ from static listings. — Most official club/facility information is not dated activity evidence.

# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Aggregator and coaching sites

Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching blogs and copied cutoff/placement tables were excluded from verified factual data because the prompt requires original official sources.

## Unsupported placement claims

Search snippets, social-media package claims, creator-reported highest packages and undated recruiter-logo pages were not imported as official placement data. The 2024-25 institute statement is preserved specifically as **job offers**, not unique students placed.

## Student rumours and accusations

Anonymous one-line allegations, personal disputes, private-individual claims and unverifiable rumours were excluded. Critical public posts are retained only as labelled anecdotes with bias limitations.

## Inaccessible or incomplete official records

The current detailed hostel-fee file, some central placement reports and the current placement brochure could not be fully opened. Their existence is recorded, but missing values remain null.

## Copyright and privacy

No long copyrighted passage, private-group content or public username was reproduced.

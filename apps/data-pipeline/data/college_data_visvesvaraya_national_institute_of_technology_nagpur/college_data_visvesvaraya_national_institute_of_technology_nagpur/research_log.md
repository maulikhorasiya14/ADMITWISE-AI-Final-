## Extraction limitations

- 16 JoSAA rows had no numeric opening/closing ranks and were excluded.
- Current hostel fee detail and some placement report links were inaccessible.
- Static club listings do not establish current activity.
- Public student evidence is self-selected and not representative.

## Assumptions avoided

- No 2026 cutoff was invented.
- No scholarship was described as guaranteed.
- Job offers were not converted into unique students placed.
- No recruiter role, package or branch eligibility was inferred.
- Missing numeric fields remain null.

Ready for human review at an estimated 84% completion. The strongest import-ready sections are identity, admission routes, 2026 seat matrix and 2023-25 cutoff records.

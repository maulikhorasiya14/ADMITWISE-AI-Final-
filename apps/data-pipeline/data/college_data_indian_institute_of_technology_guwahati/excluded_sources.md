# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source types were not used for verified factual records: Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching portals, copied cutoff tables, unsourced placement graphics and AI-generated search summaries.

## Exclusions

- **Third-party cutoff tables:** excluded because the original verified JoSAA/CSAB dataset was available.
- **Third-party fee and placement aggregators:** excluded because official JoSAA, IITG and CCD documents were available.
- **Highest-package headlines without programme/scope:** excluded unless present in an official report, and retained only as CTC.
- **Anonymous accusations or personal disputes:** excluded; no private individual is named.
- **Reddit/YouTube/GFG figures for fees, rankings, cutoffs or placements:** excluded from official data. These sources are used only for labelled qualitative experience.
- **Static recruiter logos without a year:** not used; recruiter records come from the official 2024-25 report.
- **Rumours about ragging, safety or unrestricted curfew:** not generalized. Current official policy requires manual confirmation.
- **Inaccessible/private groups and authenticated content:** not accessed or bypassed.

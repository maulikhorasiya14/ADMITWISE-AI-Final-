# Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati — AdmitWise AI College Intelligence Package

**Research date:** 2026-06-26  
**Target admission year:** 2026  
**Completion:** 93% — ready for human review

## 1. Verified college overview — official verified

IIT Guwahati is a Central Government Indian Institute of Technology, established in 1994 and governed as an Institute of National Importance. The main campus is in Guwahati 781039, Assam. The JoSAA institute code is **117**.

## 2. Accreditation and rankings — official verified / unavailable

- NIRF Engineering: **8 (2025, score 72.24)**; **7 (2024, 71.86)**; **7 (2023, 70.32)**.
- Statutory autonomous IIT and Institute of National Importance.
- Current institute-specific AICTE, NAAC and programme-level NBA records were not independently verified and remain null.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified

B.Tech admission is through JEE Advanced 2026 and JoSAA. IIT programmes use All India allocation rather than home-state/other-state quotas. Reservation, PwD provisions and female-supernumerary seats follow official rules. Branch change is discontinued from 2025.

## 4. Branches and intake — official verified

The 2026 matrix contains **12 B.Tech programmes**, **855 regular seats** and **107 female-supernumerary seats**, for **962 total seats**. See `branches.csv` and `college_profile.json` for programme-level details.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — official verified

The original verified cutoff dataset supplies all valid IIT Guwahati JoSAA records: **2023: 749**, **2024: 631**, **2025: 768**, total **2148**. Four blank 2024 no-allotment placeholders were excluded. Historical ranks are not labelled as official 2026 cutoffs and do not guarantee admission.

## 6. Fees and total cost — official verified + calculated

The full-tuition first-semester published total is ₹1,63,800 plus medical examination and insurance at actual. Calculated unchanged-rate four-year estimates are **₹11,66,200 full tuition**, **₹6,32,867 for the ₹1–5 lakh income remission band**, and **₹3,66,200 for tuition-exempt categories**. These are calculated estimates, not official fixed totals.

## 7. Scholarships — official verified

Institute Merit, Merit-cum-Means, SC-ST assistance, travel, contingency, childcare, Brotherhood Fund and Director’s Special Scholarship are documented. Statutory tuition remissions are included. Every award remains subject to current eligibility, documents and approval.

## 8. Placement statistics — institution-published

Official CCD reports cover 2022-23, 2023-24 and 2024-25. For 2024-25, 1,606 students registered and 1,001 were placed; B.Tech recorded 842 registered and 637 placed. B.Tech compensation was reported at ₹20.44 LPA median and ₹25.21 LPA average CTC, using a 632-student compensation denominator. Conflicting official counts are preserved.

## 9. Recruiters — institution-published

Thirty recent recruiter records are included from the official 2024-25 recruiter pages. Company-wise roles, eligible branches, packages and selected counts were not invented.

## 10. Company-to-skill guidance — calculated/general guidance

General preparation guidance is supplied for software, data, electronics/VLSI, core engineering and consulting/product/finance roles. It is not a verified requirement of any named recruiter.

## 11. Hostels and facilities — institution-published

The official campus page reports 11 men’s and two women’s hostels, single occupancy, rooms for all students, mess/canteen, LAN, laundry, gym, sports facilities, library and a 30-bed hospital. Current operating exceptions and detailed rules require confirmation.

## 12. Clubs and student activities — institution-associated

Twenty technical and cultural clubs are recorded. Current pages support active status for Robotics, Prakriti, E-Cell and Electronics Club; static-listed clubs are conservatively marked unable to verify where no recent dated activity was found.

## 13. Location and travel — official + map-derived

The campus is about 22 km from Guwahati Railway Station/city centre and 23 km from the airport, generally about 25–30 minutes by road under normal conditions. IITG buses connect the campus with the city centre. Coordinates are map-derived approximations.

## 14. Student-reported campus reality — qualitative

Repeated themes include a scenic residential campus, strong peer and club environment, and varied hostel/mess experiences. Evidence is qualitative and does not establish college-wide quality scores.

## 15. Parent Mode

Prioritize category-adjusted cost, branch-level placement access, scholarship verification, residential support, medical access and travel to Guwahati. Placement CTC should not be treated as take-home salary.

## 16. Student Mode

IIT Guwahati is well suited to students seeking residential campus life, research, technical clubs and a strong peer network. Branch fit matters because branch change is discontinued and recruiter eligibility differs.

## 17. Decision Risk Analysis

Major risks are historical-cutoff uncertainty, branch lock-in, changing fees, differing placement denominators, CTC-versus-base confusion, campus distance and limited independent evidence for some hostel policies.

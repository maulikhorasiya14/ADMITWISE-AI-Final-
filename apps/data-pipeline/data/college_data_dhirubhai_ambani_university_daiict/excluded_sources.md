# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Unofficial college aggregators
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar sites were excluded from verified admissions, cutoff, fee, intake, accreditation and placement records.

## Predicted All India cutoffs
Forum posts, coaching estimates, percentile-to-rank predictions and copied DA-IICT cutoff tables were excluded. No official All India JEE closing-rank archive was found, so the package does not fabricate one.

## Search snippets and AI summaries
AI-generated answers, search-engine summaries and copied tables without original documents were not used as factual records.

## Unsupported placement figures
Anonymous claims about current average, median, branch packages or company eligibility were excluded. Quantitative placement records come from NIRF and the audited 2023-24 report.

## Salary-definition mixing
Maximum Earning Potential was not relabelled as fixed salary. NIRF median, audited MEP and fixed yearly cash remain separate.

## Seat estimates
No branch intake was inferred from placement counts, NIRF historical intake, hostel capacity or cutoff movement. The 2026 totals are arithmetic sums of official All India, Gujarat and DAFS route seats.

## Scholarship guarantees
No government or institute scholarship is presented as guaranteed. Historical alumni-funded schemes are labelled cohort-specific.

## Rumours and personal accusations
Identifiable accusations, private disputes, one-line rumours and claims about individuals were excluded. Only non-identifying, paraphrased campus themes were retained.

## Absolute safety claims
Neither official security pages nor student opinion were used to claim that the locality or campus is absolutely safe or unsafe.

## Private or access-controlled sources
Private WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, closed social groups, login-only portals, deleted content and material requiring bypass were not accessed.

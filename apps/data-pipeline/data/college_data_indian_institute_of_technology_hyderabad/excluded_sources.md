# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following categories were excluded from verified factual records:

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and College Pravesh tables: third-party compilations were not used for cutoffs, fees, seat intake, accreditation or placements.
- Coaching-site cutoff predictions: not official and not labelled as 2026 ranks.
- Search-result snippets without accessible primary documents: useful only for source discovery.
- Anonymous placement tables and screenshots: excluded unless an official OCS/annual-report source could be attached.
- Student-reported branch packages in Reddit and video comments: excluded from `placements.csv` because they are not official evidence.
- Sponsored/promotional campus-tour claims: used only for observable qualitative visuals, never official quality scores.
- One-line allegations, personal disputes, rumours and claims naming private individuals: excluded entirely.
- Copied scholarship summaries without current government/institute notices: excluded from official eligibility decisions.
- Current hostel rules inferred from old student posts: excluded; rules remain a manual-verification item.

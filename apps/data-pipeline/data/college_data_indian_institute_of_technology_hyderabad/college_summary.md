# Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad — AdmitWise AI Research Summary

**Target admission year:** 2026  
**Research date:** 2026-06-26  
**Completion:** 93% — ready for human review

## 1. Verified college overview — official verified
IIT Hyderabad is a centrally funded Institute of National Importance established in 2008. The main campus is at Kandi, Sangareddy, Telangana 502284. JoSAA institute code: **107**. Sources: S002–S006.

## 2. Accreditation and rankings — official verified / unavailable
NIRF Engineering ranks: **7 in 2025 (72.31)**, **8 in 2024 (71.55)** and **8 in 2023 (70.28)**. Current AICTE, NAAC and programme-level NBA applicability/records were not independently verified and remain null. Sources: S006, S009–S011.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified
Standard UG engineering admission is through JEE Main eligibility, JEE Advanced and JoSAA. Seats use All India allocation with statutory category reservations and female-supernumerary pools. Management, institutional, TFWS and lateral-entry routes were not documented. Branch change has been discontinued from academic year 2021. Sources: S002, S005.

## 4. Branches and intake — official verified
The 2026 matrix contains **15 B.Tech programmes**, **604 regular seats**, **101 female-supernumerary seats** and **705 total seats**. `branches.csv` preserves official names and normalised branch labels. Source: S002.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — official verified
The original verified cutoff dataset supplies **2,548** valid JoSAA rows: 2023 — 871; 2024 — 740; 2025 — 937. All available programmes, rounds, categories, PwD pools and gender pools were retained. No historical rank is labelled as an official 2026 cutoff. Source: S001.

## 6. Fees and total cost — official / calculated
The July–December 2026 new-entrant fee table gives a full-tuition first-semester grand total of **INR 168,993**, including one-time fees, security deposit, recurring fees, hostel, mess advance and insurance. The unchanged-rate calculated four-year estimate is **INR 1,211,392** for full tuition, **INR 678,059** after two-thirds remission and **INR 411,392** for tuition-exempt students. These are calculated estimates, not guaranteed official totals. Source: S007.

## 7. Scholarships — official/institution-published
The package covers IIT Hyderabad MCM and SC/ST scholarships, statutory tuition exemptions/remissions, NSP schemes, Telangana ePASS and education-loan support. Every match remains potentially applicable and requires official verification. Sources: S002, S018–S021.

## 8. Placement statistics — institution-published and NIRF-reported
NIRF cohort outcomes for 2022-23 to 2024-25 are kept separate from OCS registration/offer snapshots. The 2024-25 annual report records 943 placement registrants, 700 offers, 210 hiring companies, INR 20.26 LPA average CTC and INR 66.13 LPA highest CTC. The latest 2025-26 JoSAA snapshot is provisional, not a final report. Sources: S002, S012–S016.

## 9. Recruiters — institution-published
Twenty-one recent major recruiters are stored. Roles, eligible branches, selection counts and packages remain null unless officially published. Source: S002.

## 10. Company-to-skill guidance — calculated/general
Five role-category roadmaps cover software, data, electronics/VLSI, core engineering and consulting/finance/product. They are general career guidance and not verified company requirements.

## 11. Hostels and facilities — institution-published
Official sources describe a residential UG campus, single rooms, radiant cooling, Wi-Fi/LAN, sports, medical support, labs and multiple food outlets. Current capacity, first-year exceptions, block classification, curfew and visitor rules require confirmation. Sources: S002, S022–S024.

## 12. Clubs and student activities — institution-associated
Twenty current or recently active technical, cultural, literary, service and entrepreneurship organisations are listed. Exact recruitment cycles and latest achievements are not uniformly published. Sources: S025–S029.

## 13. Location and travel — official / map-derived
The campus is in Kandi/Sangareddy rather than central Hyderabad. Official guidance cites road access from major railway stations, the airport and Miyapur metro. Coordinates and some distances are map-derived approximations. Sources: S002, S024, S030.

## 14. Student-reported campus reality — anecdotal/moderate
Eight public sources support modern infrastructure, radiant-cooled hostels, active clubs and a research-oriented environment. Repeated trade-offs are continuous academic assessment, varied mess satisfaction and substantial travel to central Hyderabad. Student placement claims were not used as official facts. Sources: S031–S038.

## 15. Parent Mode summary
Strong verified admissions, seat and fee data; substantial placement/research exposure; residential medical and hostel facilities. Verify the actual post-remission cost, final placement scope, current hostel rules and scholarship documents.

## 16. Student Mode summary
Strong fit for students seeking research, AI/semiconductor exposure, technical clubs and modern residential infrastructure. Evaluate branch interest, fractal-academic workload, non-core software access and city-distance trade-offs.

## 17. Decision Risk Analysis
Historical cutoff trends are not admission guarantees. Company eligibility varies by branch. Placement datasets use different denominators. Scholarships require documentation. The campus is outside the central city, and hostel/mess policies can change.

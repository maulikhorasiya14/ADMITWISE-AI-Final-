# Excluded Sources and Claims

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar aggregators were excluded from verified fields.
- Coaching/choice-filling sites were not used for official cutoffs, intake or eligibility.
- Search-engine and AI summaries were used only to locate original sources.
- Unofficial package claims, recruiter logos without a dated source and salary screenshots were excluded.
- Reddit and YouTube were retained only for clearly labelled qualitative experience evidence.
- A repost of the same recent hostel complaint was not counted as independent corroboration.
- Public usernames, personal accusations and absolute safety claims were excluded.
- Current donor-scholarship amounts were not inferred from old calls.
- Coordinates, traffic times, hospital distances and cost-of-living values were left null when not reliably verified.

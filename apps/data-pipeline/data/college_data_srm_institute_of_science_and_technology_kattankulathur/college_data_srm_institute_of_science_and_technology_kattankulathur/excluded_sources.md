# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Commercial college portals
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar lead-generation portals were excluded from verified numerical tables.

**Reason:** Their cutoffs, fee tables and placement figures may be copied, estimated, stale or mixed across SRM campuses.

## Unofficial SRMJEEE cutoff tables
Third-party branch closing-rank tables were not imported.

**Reason:** No original official completed-year SRM allotment export was attached. Scholarship rank bands are not admission cutoffs.

## Coaching and promotional videos
Public review and hostel/mess videos are retained only in `student_experience_sources.csv`.

**Reason:** Student identity, sponsorship and publication context are not independently verified. No numerical claims were promoted to official facts.

## Reddit placement claims
Branch package/rate claims from Reddit were excluded from `placements.csv`.

**Reason:** Denominators, eligible population, campus scope and source records are unavailable.

## Recruiter logo assumptions
A company name or logo was not interpreted as a current Kattankulathur visit unless an official dated source listed it. Even the official 2024–25 recruiter release is labelled SRM Group-level.

## Static club listings
A club was not marked active solely because it appears on an old official page.

## Search summaries and AI snippets
Search-engine snippets and generated summaries were not treated as source records.

## Rumours and personal accusations
Anonymous one-line allegations, private disputes and claims naming individuals were excluded for insufficient evidence and privacy risk.

## Private/inaccessible sources
Private groups, login-only material, access controls and CAPTCHAs were not bypassed.

# SRMIST Kattankulathur Research Log

## Scope
- College: SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur
- Target year: 2026
- Research date: 2026-06-27
- Excluded campuses: Ramapuram, Vadapalani, Trichy and Delhi-NCR unless a broader official placement/institution record was explicitly labelled

## Official portals and documents checked
- SRMIST official website and contact page
- Engineering admissions/programme/intake/fee page
- B.Tech 2026 application portal and SRMJEEE page
- 2026 Phase I counselling manual
- International admissions portal
- Accreditation/ranking page and NIRF rankings
- NIRF 2026 Engineering DCS submission
- Annual Report 2024–25
- ECE placement report
- 2026 Phase I and Phase II scholarship tables
- Scholarship categories and policy
- Boys’ and girls’ 2026–27 hostel fee circulars
- Hostel, transport, medical and student-affairs pages
- Official cultural and technical club listings

## Cutoff research
Official SRMJEEE pages, counselling instructions and public official documents were checked. No completed-year branch-wise closing-rank table satisfying the required schema was found for 2023, 2024 or 2025. Scholarship rank bands were explicitly excluded from cutoff records.

## Student-experience research
Reviewed recent Kattankulathur-specific Reddit AMAs/discussions and public campus/hostel/mess review videos. Anonymous identities were not stored. Student sources were used only for qualitative themes.

## Important findings
- 44 current Kattankulathur B.Tech programmes and 5,760 institution-published seats.
- Annual tuition range INR 2.75–4.75 lakh.
- First-year published hostel/mess examples INR 1.10–2.73 lakh.
- 17 scholarship records including phase-specific SRMJEEE awards.
- Latest NIRF placement years: 2022–23 to 2024–25.
- 30 official SRM Group recruiter examples retained with campus-scope warnings.

## Inaccessible or insufficient information
- Official 2023–25 branch closing ranks and final 2026 cutoffs
- 2026–27 AICTE approval letter and institute ID
- Category/quota seat matrix
- Kattankulathur-only branch placement report
- Company-wise branch eligibility and selection counts
- Current hostel curfew and capacity
- Current individual-club activity for most clubs

## Assumptions avoided
- No third-party cutoff substituted for official data.
- No scholarship treated as guaranteed.
- No group placement metric presented as Kattankulathur-only.
- No base-programme NBA status inherited by specialisations.
- No four-year hostel cost extrapolated.
- No static club listing treated as proof of activity.
- No safety claim stated as an absolute fact.

## Final status
Ready for human review. Cutoffs, AICTE approval, seat subcategories and Kattankulathur-only placement scope remain blocking import issues.

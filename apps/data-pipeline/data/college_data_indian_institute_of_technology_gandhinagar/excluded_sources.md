# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following were excluded from verified factual records:

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and coaching portals.
- Copied cutoff tables, because the original verified JoSAA/CSAB dataset was available.
- Third-party fee, scholarship and placement aggregators where official material was available.
- Unscoped “highest package” headlines and placement percentages without denominators.
- Static recruiter logos without a stated hiring year.
- Anonymous personal accusations, names of private individuals and unverifiable rumours.
- Reddit, YouTube and news interviews as evidence for official cutoffs, fees, scholarships, ranking or placement statistics.
- Old temporary-campus sources that could mix Chandkheda and the current Palaj campus.
- Private groups, authenticated sources and inaccessible content; no access control was bypassed.

Student sources are retained only for clearly labelled qualitative campus-reality analysis.

## 1. Verified college overview — Official verified
Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad is a centrally funded Indian Institute of Information Technology at Devghat, Jhalwa, Prayagraj. JoSAA institute code: **309**. It was established in 1999 and became an Institute of National Importance in 2014.

## 2. Accreditation and ranking — Official verified / unavailable
NIRF Engineering 2025: rank band 101–150. NIRF Engineering 2024: rank 87, score 45.84. Current authoritative NAAC and programme-wise NBA validity remain unverified.

## 3. Admission routes — Official verified
UG admission is through JEE Main and JoSAA, followed by CSAB Special for vacancies. DASA/CIWG routes apply under current rules. The 2026 matrix is All India.

## 4. Branches and intake — Official verified
The 2026 matrix contains six programmes, **461 total seats**, consisting of **373 regular seat-capacity positions and 88 female-supernumerary seats**. See `branches.csv` and `college_profile.json`.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — Official verified
The package contains **761** validated rows for 2023–2025: 723 JoSAA and 38 CSAB Special. JoSAA category ranks and CSAB All India CRL are kept separate. 3 blank-rank JoSAA combinations were excluded.

## 6. Fees and total cost — Official verified / calculated
The 2026 B.Tech schedule is structured in `fees.csv`. For double-sharing accommodation, calculated four-year published totals excluding mess are **₹10,62,760 for Gen/OBC/EWS**, **₹2,70,760 for SC/ST/PwD**, and **₹7,70,760 for DASA-CIWG**. These are sums of official semester totals, not separate institute-published grand totals; fees are subject to annual revision. Mess is explicitly variable and is not estimated.

## 7. Scholarships — Official verified / institution-published
Nine structured records cover State schemes, disability schemes, SC/ST/OBC-EBC-DNT top-class schemes, the Central Sector scholarship and two institute merit schemes. Every benefit is conditional and requires official verification; no award is guaranteed.

## 8. Placement statistics — Official verified and institution-published
NIRF records: 2022–23: 372/384 placed, median ₹30 LPA; 2023–24: 348/428, median ₹18.5 LPA; 2024–25: 433/440, median ₹26 LPA. The placement brochure separately reports 422/431 registered students placed, average ₹33 LPA and highest ₹120 LPA. The JoSAA institute profile reports 419/431 who opted. All scopes and the 419-versus-422 discrepancy are preserved rather than merged.

## 9. Recruiters — Official verified
Thirty 2024–25 companies and their published selection counts are included from the JoSAA institute profile. Roles, branch eligibility, CGPA cut-offs and packages are not inferred.

## 11. Hostels and facilities — Institution-published

## 12. Clubs — Institution-associated

## 13. Location and travel — Official/map-derived
The campus is in Jhalwa. The official profile states it is about 10 km from the city centre, 7 km from Prayagraj Junction and about 7 km from the airport. Travel times remain estimates.

## 14. Student-reported campus reality — Anecdotal
Public evidence commonly highlights coding and placement culture, but the current source set is search-level. Hostel quality, workload, senior-junior interaction and branch-specific software access need direct recent-student verification.

## 15. Parent Mode
Identity, seat matrix, cutoffs, official fees, scholarship possibilities and NIRF placement cohorts are well sourced. Confirm mess costs, hostel rules and the placement-count discrepancy.

## 16. Student Mode
The institute is strongly technology-focused, with verified IT/ECE/IT-BI programmes and extensive placement participation. Verify branch workload, current clubs, hostel reality and company eligibility before deciding.

## 17. Decision Risk Analysis
- Placement sources use different cohort definitions and report 419, 422 and 433 placed students; do not compare percentages without scope labels.
- Mess cost is not fixed in the official schedule, so a four-year living total cannot be guaranteed.
- Current NAAC/NBA records were not verified.
- Recruiter presence does not establish role, branch eligibility or future participation.
- Qualitative campus evidence remains limited and should not be treated as college-wide fact.

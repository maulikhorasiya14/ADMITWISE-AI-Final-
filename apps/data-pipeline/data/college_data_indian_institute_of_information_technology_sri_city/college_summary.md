## 1. Verified college overview — official verified

IIIT Sri City is an autonomous public-private-partnership Indian Institute of Information Technology established in 2013 and designated an Institute of National Importance. The official 2026 JoSAA institute code is **307**. The permanent residential campus is at 630 Gnan Marg, Sri City, Tirupati district, Andhra Pradesh, on an officially reported 81-acre site.

## 2. Accreditation — official verified and unavailable

Institute of National Importance and autonomous/PPP status are verified. A current NAAC grade, programme-wise NBA validity and AICTE institute record were not verified and remain null. A 2026 NIRF Engineering institution submission was found, but no exact published rank was inferred.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified

B.Tech admissions use JEE Main through JoSAA, followed by CSAB Special rounds if vacancies exist. All regular programmes use All India quota. Female-supernumerary and category/PwD pools follow the official seat matrix. The institute states that **branch change is no longer allowed**.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — official verified

The supplied verified JoSAA dataset contains **576** numeric IIIT Sri City records: 182 from 2023, 160 from 2024 and 234 from 2025. The official CSAB PDFs add **62** 2025 records: 39 in Special Round 1 and 23 in Special Round 2. No IIIT Sri City record was found in the supplied Special Round 3 PDF. Total numeric cutoff rows: **638**.

JoSAA reserved-category ranks retain their category-rank semantics. Every CSAB rank is labelled **All India CRL** exactly as stated in the official PDFs. JoSAA and CSAB must not be compared as though their reserved-category rank meanings were identical.

## 6. Fees and total cost — official verified and calculated

The 2026-27 schedule publishes tuition of ₹1,85,000 per semester, hostel establishment fee of ₹22,000 per semester, mess fee of ₹22,000 per semester, ₹17,000 admission fee, ₹20,000 refundable caution deposit, ₹2,000 alumni fee and ₹3,000 annual medical/insurance charge. The first-year total is ₹5,00,000 including the refundable deposit.

A straight-line four-year institutional estimate is **₹18,83,000**, including the refundable deposit but excluding personal costs. This is a calculated estimate, not an official total, and the institute explicitly states that fees may be revised from the second year onward.

## 7. Scholarships and financial assistance — official verified, award not guaranteed

Eligibility, benefit, deadline and renewal must be checked against live 2026-27 notices.

## 14. Student-reported campus reality — student-reported

Repeated themes support a strong academic/coding focus, project culture and placement preparation. Students also repeatedly describe limited outside-city life. Hostel infrastructure receives mixed reports across buildings and years. Placement discussions often debate denominator and eligibility definitions; those opinions are not treated as official evidence.

## 15. Parent Mode summary

The strongest verified positives are statutory status, focused programmes, residential infrastructure and high college-reported placement averages. Major checks are fee escalation, placement denominator, branch lock-in, hostel construction and scheme-level scholarship eligibility.

## 16. Student Mode summary

The college suits students seeking an intensive technology environment, early coding/projects, research and internships. Students wanting metropolitan life, low fees, broad branch choice or easy branch switching may find the fit weaker.

## 17. Decision Risk Analysis

Key risks: high four-year cost, provisional Batch 2026 placement data, registered-cohort placement percentages, no branch change, evolving hostel construction, remote industrial-town lifestyle, uncertain scholarship awards, and unverified company-level ECE/software eligibility.

# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source categories were excluded from verified factual tables unless they linked directly to an original official document:

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and College Pravesh: third-party aggregators; figures can be stale or copied.
- Coaching/admission-prediction websites: not authoritative for official cutoffs, seats or eligibility.
- AI-generated search summaries and copied tables: provenance and extraction quality are uncertain.
- Reddit/YouTube placement package claims: retained only as student perceptions; official placement-page figures are used for statistics.
- Anonymous allegations of placement manipulation or administrative misconduct: excluded as factual claims because they are unverifiable and potentially harmful.
- Undated recruiter-logo collages: excluded from year-specific recruiter confirmation.
- Personal disputes, named-private-individual accusations and rumours: excluded entirely.
- Private groups, login-only sources and inaccessible communications: not accessed.

Student sources are included only in `student_experience_sources.csv` and `campus_reality.json` with D/E confidence labels.

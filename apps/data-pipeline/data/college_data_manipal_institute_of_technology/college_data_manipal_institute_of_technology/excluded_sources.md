# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Commercial college portals
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar lead-generation portals were excluded from verified tables because figures may be copied, estimated, undated or campus-mixed.

## Coaching/review videos
Two videos are retained only as anecdotal student-experience sources. Reviewer identity and numerical provenance were not verified.

## Reddit placement compilation
The 2026 community branch-wise placement table is excluded from `placements.csv` because its source data, eligibility population and completeness are not official.

## Applicant questions
Question-led threads were used only to discover manual checks; questions are not evidence.

## Search/AI summaries
Search snippets and AI summaries were not treated as sources.

## Unsupported claims
Branch-wise package claims, 100% placement claims without denominators, undated recruiter logos interpreted as current visits, unofficial cutoffs, rumours, personal disputes and private-group content were excluded.

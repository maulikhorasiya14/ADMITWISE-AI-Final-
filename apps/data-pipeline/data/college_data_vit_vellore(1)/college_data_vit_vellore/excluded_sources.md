# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Unofficial cutoff and fee tables
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching sites and social-media rank charts were excluded from verified cutoff, seat and fee data. VIT's own FAQ warns that branch rank ranges vary and are only tentatively understood through counselling.

## Search snippets and AI summaries
Generated summaries, answer boxes and copied tables without traceable primary sources were excluded.

## Individual allotment screenshots
A candidate's counselling result does not establish an official closing rank for an entire branch/category and was excluded.

## Unsupported placement claims
Anonymous package claims, branch-wise spreadsheets, influencer graphics and forum anecdotes were excluded. Official offer counts were not treated as unique students placed.

## Campus mixing
Chennai, AP and Bhopal programme, hostel and student-life records were excluded from Vellore facts. Centralized placement sources are explicitly labelled all-campus or institution-level.

## Seat estimates
No 2026 seat number was inferred from 2024-25 intake, hostel capacity, classroom size, applicant count or counselling behaviour.

## Hostel and discipline rumours
Personal accusations, one-line allegations, private-group messages and unverifiable incident narratives were excluded. Only non-identifying repeated qualitative themes were retained.

## Private or inaccessible material
Login-only counselling details, VTOP pages, private WhatsApp/Telegram/Discord groups, deleted content, paywalls and access-controlled documents were not bypassed.

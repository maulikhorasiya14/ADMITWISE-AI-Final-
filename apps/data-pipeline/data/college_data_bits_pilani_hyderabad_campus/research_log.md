# Research Log — BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus

**Research date:** 2026-06-27  
**Target year:** 2026

## Searches and portals checked
- BITS Pilani and Hyderabad-campus official sites
- BITSAT 2026 admission, eligibility, programme, cutoff, fee and scholarship pages
- NIRF and official accreditation pages
- Hyderabad Placement Unit, placement-statistics PDF and recruiter PDF
- Student Welfare, hostel/facility, medical and transport pages
- Official clubs, associations, publications and festival archives
- National Scholarship Portal
- Recent public Reddit discussions and campus-tour videos
- Map-derived connectivity and nearby ecosystem estimates

## Documents reviewed
- Hyderabad Placement Statistics for Three Years
- List of Recruiters for Academic Year 2023-24
- Official historical BITSAT score page
- Official 2026-27 fee and scholarship material
- Official campus, welfare, facility and medical pages

## Qualitative research
Thirteen public student-experience sources were catalogued across hostel life, mess, heat, onboarding, grading, city travel, senior support, campus development and branch-to-role concerns. Usernames were not stored.

## Limitations
- No official seat matrix
- No verified current campus-specific AICTE/NBA table
- No final 2026 cutoff
- No B.E.-branch-wise placement data
- No recruiter roles, eligibility, packages or selected counts
- Current hostel counts/capacity not verified
- Many clubs lack recent dated activity

## Assumptions avoided
- No seat estimate
- No predicted cutoff labelled official
- No projected future fee increase
- No inferred recruiter role or eligibility
- No student opinion converted into official fact
- No absolute safety claim
- No old club listing treated as proof of current activity
- No university-wide statistic presented as Hyderabad-only

## Final status
The 18-file package is ready for human review. Identity, admission routes, programmes, completed cutoffs, fees, campus-level placements, recruiters and location are structured. Intake, current accreditation, branch-level placements, hostel capacity/rules and several club records remain verification items.

# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Unofficial aggregators
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar sites were not used for verified cutoffs, fees, intake, accreditation or placement figures.

## Search snippets and AI summaries
Generated summaries, answer boxes and copied tables without traceable primary sources were excluded.

## Unsupported placement claims
Anonymous branch-wise packages, social-media graphics and forum anecdotes were excluded. Quantitative placement records come only from the official Hyderabad-campus PDF.

## Broad marketing claim
The placement page's “almost 100%” wording was not used as a numeric result because the detailed 2023-24 report gives lower percentages. It is preserved as a conflict.

## Predicted cutoffs
Coaching-site and forum predictions for 2026 were excluded. Only completed official 2023–2025 scores are retained.

## Estimated seats
No seat count was inferred from annual admissions, hostel counts, class size, placement registrations or cutoff movement.

## Rumours and personal accusations
One-line allegations, identifiable personal disputes, private-group messages and unsupported incidents were excluded. Only non-identifying qualitative themes were retained.

## Campus mixing
University-wide NAAC/NIRF records are labelled university-wide. Pilani/Goa placement, hostel, club and location records were not imported as Hyderabad facts.

## Private or inaccessible sources
Login-only groups, private Discord/WhatsApp/Telegram content, deleted posts, paywalls and access-controlled material were not accessed.

# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source classes were deliberately excluded from verified factual records:

- **Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and College Pravesh:** useful as discovery leads but not authoritative for official fees, cutoffs, placements, intake or admission rules.
- **Coaching and exam-preparation websites:** copied cutoff tables and predictions were not imported.
- **Unsourced blogs and AI-generated search summaries:** no figures were accepted without reaching the originating official document.
- **Third-party news reports about salaries or placement percentages:** excluded when an official placement report was available or when campus scope was unclear.
- **Undated recruiter-logo pages:** not used to claim current participation. Recruiters were retained only from the dated official 2024-25 Pilani list.
- **Anonymous one-line allegations and personal disputes:** excluded from qualitative synthesis.
- **Rumours naming private students, faculty or staff:** excluded to avoid unverified personal accusations.
- **Private groups, login-only pages and inaccessible discussions:** not bypassed or scraped.
- **Map values without a traceable approved source:** exact coordinates and several local travel times remain null.
- **Predicted 2026 cutoffs:** excluded. Historical 2023-2025 final scores are preserved only as historical evidence.

Student-experience sources that were retained are separately labelled D/E and are never used for official facts.

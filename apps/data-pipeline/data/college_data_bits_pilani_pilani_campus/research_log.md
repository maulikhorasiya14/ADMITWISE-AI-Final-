## Student-experience review

- 15 public Reddit discussions covering mess, hostel, heat, location, coding, clubs, ragging, academics and social life

- No usernames were retained.
- No upvote count was treated as proof.
- Themes were coded as repeated, limited or conflicting signals.

## Extraction limitations

- No public 2026 branch-wise seat matrix was found.
- Final 2026 cutoffs had not concluded/published as of the research date.
- Official cutoff data uses final BITSAT scores, not opening/closing ranks.
- Newest placement statistics are university-wide, while earlier records are Pilani-campus-specific.
- Recruiter list does not disclose role, branch eligibility, package or selection counts.
- Most club listings do not prove current activity.
- Current hostel capacity, occupancy, room type and curfew were not publicly verified.
- AICTE ID and current NBA programme validity were not verified.

## Assumptions avoided

- No intake was estimated from cutoff, enrolment or placement counts.
- No predicted 2026 cutoff was generated.
- No four-year hostel/mess total was guessed.
- No recruiter logo was treated as evidence of a specific role or package.
- No single student post was generalized to the full campus.
- No prohibited aggregator figure was imported.

## Final status

Ready for human review. Structured sections are usable with explicit scope labels, while seat matrix, current statutory programme accreditation, final 2026 cutoff, campus-specific 2024-25 placement and several operational details remain verification blockers.

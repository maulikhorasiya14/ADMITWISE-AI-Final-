# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Prohibited aggregators
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching sites and copied cutoff tables were not used for verified facts. The original user-confirmed cutoff CSV and official JoSAA/NIRF/IIT Delhi sources take precedence.

## Separate campus exclusions
- Four IIT Delhi Abu Dhabi programmes and their 40 seats were excluded from the New Delhi seat matrix.
- 126 historical cutoff rows whose programme name explicitly identified the Abu Dhabi campus were excluded.

## Unsupported placement claims
- Unofficial highest-package, average-package and branch-wise placement figures were excluded.
- Company logos or generic recruiter lists without a year were not treated as proof of current participation.
- OCS offer snapshots were not converted into placement percentages.

## Hostel and campus rumours
Anonymous claims about food poisoning, dirty water, unsafe conditions, curfew or universal hostel guarantees were not treated as official facts. Limited public discussions are retained only as labelled anecdotal evidence.

## Accreditation assumptions
AICTE, UGC, NAAC or NBA status was not inferred from IIT Delhi's statutory status. Unverified fields remain null and require manual confirmation if the application schema demands them.

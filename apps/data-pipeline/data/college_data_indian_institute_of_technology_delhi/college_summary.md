# Indian Institute of Technology Delhi — AdmitWise AI College Intelligence Report

**Target admission year:** 2026  
**Research date:** 2026-06-26  
**Package scope:** IIT Delhi **New Delhi campus only**. Abu Dhabi programmes and 126 historical cutoff rows were deliberately excluded to avoid campus mixing.  
**Completion estimate:** 92% — ready for human review, not direct unreviewed publication.

## 1. Verified college overview — official verified

IIT Delhi is an autonomous Central Government statutory institution established in 1961 and declared an Institute of National Importance in 1963. The New Delhi campus is at Hauz Khas, New Delhi 110016. JoSAA institute code: **104**.

## 2. Accreditation and ranking — official verified / unavailable

- Institution of National Importance and autonomous statutory IIT: verified.
- Institution of Eminence status: verified from 2018.
- NIRF Engineering: Rank 2 in 2023, 2024 and 2025; 2025 score 85.74.
- AICTE institute-specific approval, NAAC grade and NBA programme records: left null rather than inferred.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified

UG admission is through JEE Advanced and JoSAA under All India seat allocation. There is no home-state, management, institutional or TFWS route in the published matrix. Branch change is possible after the first year within the New Delhi campus subject to academic and conduct conditions; it is not guaranteed, and cross-campus change is prohibited.

## 4. Branches and intake — official verified

The 2026 New Delhi seat matrix contains **19 programmes and 1,209 seats**. Four separate Abu Dhabi programmes with 40 seats are excluded. Detailed gender/category pools are retained in `college_profile.json`.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — official verified

The package contains **3,349 verified JoSAA rows** from the original user-confirmed cutoff dataset:

- 2023: 1,117
- 2024: 960
- 2025: 1,272

All rounds, programmes, categories, PwD pools and gender pools present in the verified dataset are retained. No official 2026 cutoff is claimed.

## 6. Fees and total cost — official verified + calculated

Published 2026-27 semester tuition is ₹1,00,000 for General/EWS/OBC students above ₹5 lakh family income, ₹33,333 for the ₹1–5 lakh band, and nil for below ₹1 lakh and SC/ST/PwD students. The institute also publishes one-time, other, refundable and residence-seat components. Calculated four-year institutional estimates in `fees.csv` exclude mess, separate BHM charges, personal spending and future revisions.

## 7. Scholarships — institution-published

The package records Merit-cum-Means, Merit Prize, Free Studentship, SC/ST/PwD tuition exemption and eligible SC/ST free-messing/pocket-allowance support. All matching must remain “potentially applicable” until current forms, deadlines and documents are checked.

## 8. Placement statistics — official verified / institution-published

NIRF 2026 submission provides the latest three completed years. For 2024-25, UG four-year programmes report 986 graduating in minimum time, 818 placed, median ₹20 LPA and 73 higher studies; UG five-year programmes report 110 graduating, 103 placed, median ₹30 LPA and six higher studies. OCS snapshots are retained separately because offer counts and unique selections use different scopes.

## 9. Recruiters — institution-associated

Twenty-seven companies making double-digit offers in 2025-26 are recorded from OCS. Roles, branch eligibility, exact company-wise selections and packages were not published and remain blank.

## 10. Company-to-skill guidance — calculated/general guidance

General preparation guidance is included for software, data/analytics, consulting/product, finance/quant, electronics/VLSI, mechanical/manufacturing and other core engineering domains. It is not represented as a verified company requirement.

## 11. Hostels and facilities — official / institution-associated

The residential ecosystem is large and student-governed, but institute-wide capacity is constrained and sharing is common. IIT Delhi has sports, pool, courts, hospital, library, banks, shopping, technical facilities and accessibility support. Exact 2026-27 BHM and mess charges require manual confirmation.

## 12. Clubs and student activities — institution-associated

Twenty cultural, technical, publication, welfare, service and entrepreneurship organisations are listed. Board-level activity is clearly current; activity dates and recruitment for every individual club still require checking.

## 13. Location and travel — official + map-derived

IIT Delhi has a Magenta Line metro station. New Delhi Railway Station is approximately 14 km away and IGI Airport roughly 12 km, subject to terminal and route. AIIMS and South Delhi amenities are nearby. Delhi-NCR provides strong corporate/startup exposure but off-campus costs are high.

## 14. Student-reported campus reality — student-reported / anecdotal

Repeated evidence points to strong peer networks and extensive opportunities but meaningful academic-balance pressure. Hostel and mess experiences vary materially by building and year; isolated claims about water, food or cleanliness are preserved as anecdotal, not college-wide facts.

## 15. Parent Mode summary

Strong source-backed academic reputation, placement medians, transport, hospital access and category-linked tuition relief. Verify current hostel/mess charges, room allocation and scholarship paperwork before budgeting.

## 16. Student Mode summary

Choose based on branch curriculum, not branch-change hopes. The campus provides exceptional clubs, projects, recruiters and Delhi exposure, but students should be prepared for workload and variable hostel conditions.

## 17. Decision Risk Analysis

Key risks are assuming branch change, comparing incomparable placement denominators, under-budgeting BHM/mess costs, expecting uniform hostel quality and underestimating workload or Delhi environmental/cost factors.

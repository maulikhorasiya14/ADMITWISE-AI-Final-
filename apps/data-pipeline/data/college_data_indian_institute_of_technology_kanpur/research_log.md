# Research Log — IIT Kanpur

## Scope
Executed the user-provided AdmitWise AI specification for Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, target admission year 2026.

## Official portals and documents checked
- JoSAA 2026 institute profile and category-wise seat matrix.
- JEE Advanced 2026 information brochure and admission criteria.
- Original verified merged JoSAA/CSAB cutoff dataset for 2023–2025.
- IIT Kanpur 2026–27 first-semester fee circular.
- NIRF Engineering rankings for 2023, 2024 and 2025 and IIT Kanpur NIRF outcome submission.
- IIT Kanpur annual/convocation reports for 2022–23, 2023–24 and 2024–25.
- SPO placement portal/proformas.
- SSPC financial-aid, MCM, FBM, INSPIRE, donor and scholarship calendar pages.
- Official health, counselling, visitor, library, student-life, SIIC and transport pages.

## Cutoff extraction
- Matched institute names after whitespace normalization, including the double-spaced 2023 spelling.
- Retained 2,530 valid rows across all available rounds.
- Excluded 4 blank no-allotment placeholders.
- JoSAA and CSAB remain separate; IIT Kanpur has no CSAB Special Round records.

## Qualitative research
Reviewed eight public student/student-media sources covering clubs, mess systems, hostel capacity, campus movement, academic adjustment and peer life. Evidence remains labelled D/E and was not used for official numeric facts.

## Assumptions avoided
- No 2026 cutoff prediction was inserted.
- No variable mess food cost was estimated.
- No company role, package or branch eligibility was inferred from a recruiter name.
- No numeric campus-culture rating was created.
- AICTE/NAAC/NBA values remain null where not verified.

## Limitations and unresolved issues
- Official 2026 JoSAA cutoffs are not yet available and have not been predicted as official.
- Final 2024-25 and 2025-26 placement reports with package statistics, roles and company-wise selection counts are not public in a complete form.
- The 2026 fee circular publishes approximate hostel MEC but not the variable food/mess bill.
- Current first-year hostel sharing, exact capacity and allotment guarantee require DoSA confirmation.
- AICTE, NAAC and programme-level NBA records were not verified; IIT Kanpur is a statutory Institute of National Importance.
- Current scholarship deadlines and notified external-scheme institute lists need 2026-27 confirmation.
- Recent dated activity was not independently verified for every club.
- Campus-reality evidence is self-selected and insufficient for numeric ratings.

## Final status
All 18 required files were generated. The package is ready for human review, with high-confidence admissions/cutoff/seat data and explicit warnings for provisional placement, hostel, scholarship and qualitative fields.

## 1. Verified college overview — official verified
IIT Kanpur is a Central Government statutory Indian Institute of Technology established in 1959 and an Institute of National Importance. The main residential campus is at Kalyanpur, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh 208016. This package does not mix records from any outreach or extension location.

## 2. Accreditation and rankings — official verified / unavailable
IIT Kanpur was ranked **4th in NIRF Engineering** in 2023, 2024 and 2025, with scores of 80.65, 82.79 and 81.82 respectively. The NIRF institution ID is **IR-E-I-1075**. Separate current AICTE, NAAC and programme-level NBA records were not verified and remain null rather than inferred.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified
Admission to the 2026 JoSAA-listed UG programmes is through JEE Main eligibility, JEE Advanced 2026 and JoSAA. There is no home-state quota, management quota or CSAB Special Round for IIT seats. The Class XII criterion is 75% aggregate (65% for SC/ST/PwD) or category-wise top 20 percentile, with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics compulsory. Branch change is allowed once after the second semester subject to CPI order and programme constraints.

## 4. Branches and intake — official verified
The 2026 matrix contains **15 programmes**, **988 regular-capacity seats** and **252 female-supernumerary seats**, for **1,240 total seats**. Intelligent Systems is new in the 2026 matrix and therefore has no historical cutoff rows in 2023–2025.

## 6. Fees and total cost — institution-published / calculated
The official 2026–27 first-semester circular lists hosteller totals of ₹1,57,166 for full-tuition students, ₹90,499 for the one-third-tuition income band, ₹57,166 for zero-tuition MEB students, and ₹54,666 for SC/ST students under the stated waiver. The calculated full-tuition four-year institutional/MEC estimate is **₹10,38,928**, assuming unchanged rates across eight semesters. This excludes food/mess consumption, personal costs and future revisions and is not an official published programme total.

# Excluded Sources and Claims — IIT Kanpur

## Prohibited aggregators
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching sites and copied cutoff tables were not used for verified admissions, fee or placement data. The original verified merged cutoff dataset was used instead.

## Unsupported placement claims
Search-result snippets and social posts claiming current average/highest packages, 100% placement or branch-wise software access were excluded unless present in an official report. Company-logo pages were not treated as proof of current visits.

## Unsupported hostel and campus claims
One-line reviews, rumours about curfew, absolute mess-quality ratings and safety claims were excluded. Historical hostel-capacity reporting is retained only as student-reported evidence and not as a current official capacity fact.

## Private or inaccessible sources
Private groups, sign-in-only content, personal accusations and identifiable private-student disputes were excluded. No access controls or CAPTCHAs were bypassed.

## Predictions
No predicted cutoff, placement, scholarship, fee or hostel charge is labelled official. General career guidance is separated from college-specific facts.

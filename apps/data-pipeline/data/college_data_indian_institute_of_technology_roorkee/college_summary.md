## 1. Verified college overview — official verified

IIT Roorkee is a Central Government statutory Indian Institute of Technology and Institute of National Importance, tracing its origin to 1847 and converted to an IIT on 21 September 2001. JoSAA institute code: **113**. The package covers the main Roorkee campus only.

## 2. Accreditation and rankings — official verified / unavailable

## 3. Admission routes — official verified

UG entry is through JEE Advanced and JoSAA after meeting the applicable JEE Main/Class XII eligibility. Seats use All India quota with category/PwD reservations and female-supernumerary pools. Branch change is available after the first Autumn semester for eligible JEE Advanced entrants except B.Arch./B.Des., but is competitive and must not be assumed.

## 6. Fees and total cost — official / calculated

The 2026-27 fee circular lists tuition of INR 100,000 per semester for the full-tuition category, INR 33,333 for the INR 1-5 lakh income band, and nil tuition for SC/ST/PwD and eligible students below INR 1 lakh family income. Calculated unchanged-rate four-year totals are:

- Full tuition: **INR 1,133,400**
- One-third tuition: **INR 600,064**
- Tuition-exempt: **INR 333,400**

These are calculations, not official fixed totals. Mess is an advance and future fee revisions are excluded.

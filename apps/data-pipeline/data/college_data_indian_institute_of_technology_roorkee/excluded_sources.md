# Excluded Sources and Claims

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and coaching sites were not used as verified factual sources because official JoSAA, NIRF and IIT Roorkee sources were available.
- Unsourced salary/package tables and copied recruiter lists were excluded.
- Search-engine and AI summaries were not registered as sources.
- Anonymous accusations, personal disputes, rumours and claims naming private individuals were excluded.
- One-line hostel/mess comments were not generalized.
- Private groups, paywalls, sign-in-only pages and access controls were not bypassed.
- Historical Polymer programme placement rows are retained only as historical placement evidence and are not presented as current 2026 admissions programmes.

# Excluded Sources and Claims

Aggregator sites such as Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching sites and copied tables were excluded from verified factual data. Anonymous one-line accusations, private-group content, unsupported package claims and rumours about hostel or faculty conditions were excluded. Public Reddit, YouTube and map material is retained only as labelled qualitative evidence. No official 2026 cutoff, guaranteed scholarship, recruiter role, branch eligibility or final 2026 fee was invented.

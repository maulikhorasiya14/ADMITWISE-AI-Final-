# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following categories were not used as verified factual evidence:

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and other commercial aggregators: useful for discovery only; figures were not imported because original official sources are available.
- Coaching and counselling websites: cutoff, fee and placement summaries were excluded unless traceable to an official document.
- Search-engine/AI snippets: not treated as records.
- Anonymous claims that specific administrators, faculty or private individuals acted improperly: excluded to avoid repeating unverified personal accusations.
- Unverified claims that most high packages are off-campus or restricted by gender: retained only as a qualitative transparency concern, never as placement data.
- Historical company-result images from 2019-20: not used as current recruiter evidence.
- Exact numeric placement averages quoted only by third-party sites: excluded.
- Rumours about ragging, safety, relationships or disciplinary actions without multiple reliable sources: excluded.

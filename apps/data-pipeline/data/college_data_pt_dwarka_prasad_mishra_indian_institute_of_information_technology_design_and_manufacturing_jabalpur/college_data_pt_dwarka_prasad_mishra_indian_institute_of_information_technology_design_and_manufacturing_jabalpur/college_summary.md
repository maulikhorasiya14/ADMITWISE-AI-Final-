## 1. Verified college overview — official verified
PDPM IIITDM Jabalpur is a centrally funded Institute of National Importance established in January 2005. The official JoSAA 2026 institute code is **311**. The permanent, fully residential campus is on Dumna Airport Road, Jabalpur.

## 2. Accreditation and ranking — official verified / unavailable
NIRF Engineering: **101–150 band in 2025**, **101–150 band in 2024**, and **rank 97 with score 42.14 in 2023**. Current NAAC grade, current programme-wise NBA validity and AICTE applicability were not conclusively verified and remain null.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified
B.Tech admission is through JEE Main and JoSAA. CSAB Special is the vacancy-filling route. The 2026 matrix uses the All India quota. Branch change after the second semester is vacancy/CPI based, with a special provision for the top five B.Tech students.

## 4. Branches and intake — official verified
The 2026 JoSAA matrix has **4 B.Tech programmes and 550 seats**: CSE 275, ECE 137, Mechanical 69 and Smart Manufacturing 69. Female-only seat pools are included in the 550-seat total; the matrix shows **zero female-supernumerary seats**.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — official verified
The package contains **949 numeric cutoff rows**: **900 JoSAA** rows for 2023–2025 and **49 CSAB Special 2025** rows. CSAB Round 1 has no institute row, Round 2 has 26 rows and Round 3 has 23 rows. Six blank-rank JoSAA 2024 rows were excluded rather than converted to zero. CSAB ranks are All India CRL and must remain separate from JoSAA reserved-category ranks.

## 6. Fees and total cost — official verified + calculated
For the 2026 batch, Gen/OBC/EWS students pay ₹71,750 tuition, ₹7,500 academic fee and ₹11,000 hostel fee per semester. SC/ST/PwD students are tuition-exempt. The first-semester institute total is ₹105,750 for Gen/OBC/EWS and ₹34,000 for SC/ST/PwD. A ₹15,000 mess advance is deposited separately each semester and adjusted to actual. Straight-line four-year estimates are approximately **₹8.58 lakh** and **₹2.84 lakh**, respectively, including the refundable ₹5,000 caution deposit and current mess advances; these are calculations, not official guaranteed totals.

## 7. Scholarships — official / potentially applicable
The institute lists MCM and Single Parent scholarships and links a SPACS manual covering MCM, freeships, book grants and SC/ST boarding/lodging support. The manual contains legacy numeric values; 2026-27 benefits and income ceilings require current-notice verification. NSP and Madhya Pradesh schemes are included as potentially applicable, never guaranteed.

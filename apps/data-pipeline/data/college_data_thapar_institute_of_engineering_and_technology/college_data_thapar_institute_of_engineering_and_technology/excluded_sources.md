# Excluded Sources and Claims

## Commercial college portals
Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching sites and similar lead-generation pages were not used for verified cutoffs, fees, placements, intake, accreditation or admission rules.

**Reason:** figures are often copied, estimated, stale, campus-mixed or disconnected from an original official document.

## Unofficial 2023 cutoff tables
Third-party tables and document-sharing copies were encountered but excluded.

**Reason:** no original TIET-issued 2023 round-wise PDF was verified. `cutoffs.csv` therefore transparently lacks 2023 instead of using an unofficial substitute.

## Community branch-wise 2026 placement compilation
A Reddit table was reviewed for qualitative transparency concerns but excluded from `placements.csv`.

**Reason:** source population, eligibility denominator, timing and completeness are not official or independently verifiable.

## Review videos and applicant discussions
Public YouTube reviews and Reddit discussions were retained only as qualitative evidence.

**Reason:** identities, sponsorship, methodology and numeric source data are uncertain. No unofficial package, cutoff, fee or placement number entered verified records.

## Undated recruiter claims
Official recruiter names were retained, but undated logo/name listings were not converted into confirmed current visits.

**Reason:** a historical or undated listing does not prove 2026 participation, role, branch eligibility or package.

## Questions treated as non-evidence
Applicant questions about curfew, Wi-Fi, rooms or placement were used only to identify verification needs.

**Reason:** a question is not proof that a condition exists.

## Rumours, accusations and private identities
Unsupported accusations, personal disputes and private names were excluded.

**Reason:** weak evidence, privacy risk and no reliable value for structured admissions guidance.

## Search snippets and AI summaries
Search-engine or AI-generated summaries were not treated as source records.

**Reason:** they can be truncated, stale or detached from the underlying document.

# TIET Patiala Research Log

- Campus: Patiala only
- Target admission year: 2026
- Research date: 2026-06-27
- Excluded: Derabassi/LMTSM and COSE records

- 2026-27 scholarship policy
- 2026 hostel facilities and student-activities documents
- Official 2026 Round 1 cutoff PDF

## Cutoff extraction
- Parsed official route, round, programme, category, quota, AIR/PCM score and percentile.
- Excluded Derabassi COSE rows.
- Kept 2024 AIML separate from 2026 AI & DS.
- Kept opening rank null because the official tables provide cutoff/closing values only.
- 2026 is partial Round 1 and is not labelled final.
- Repeated searches did not locate an official 2023 cutoff PDF; third-party values were excluded.

## Placement extraction
- Used NIRF overall UG four-year records for 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24.
- Calculated placed/graduating percentages and labelled them calculated.
- Did not use community branch placement tables as verified outcomes.

## Qualitative research
- Reviewed public Reddit AMAs/discussions on campus life, societies, hostel, branch choice, placements and local transport.
- Reviewed public hostel tours/review videos for direct observable context.
- Did not store public usernames or repeat unsupported personal accusations.
- Classified repeated, limited and conflicting signals rather than assigning a numeric culture score.

## Inaccessible or unresolved information
- Official 2023 cutoff series
- Complete/final 2026 counselling series
- 2026-27 AICTE EoA
- Category-wise seat matrix
- Programme-level NBA validity letters
- Current branch-wise placement report
- Dated recruiter-by-year evidence
- Confirmed Patiala hostel session/rates and bed capacity
- Current dated activity for every club

## Assumptions avoided
- No unofficial cutoff substituted for 2023.
- No historical AIML record automatically mapped to current AI & DS.
- No scholarship treated as guaranteed.
- No hostel inflation forecast presented as fact.
- No company treated as a confirmed 2026 recruiter from an undated list.
- No student report used for official fees, cutoffs, accreditation, intake or placement outcomes.
- No safety or culture absolute inferred from one anonymous account.

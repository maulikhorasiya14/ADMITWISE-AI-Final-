The 2026 admission document lists **18 undergraduate engineering programmes with 3,390 sanctioned seats**. Derabassi/LMTSM and its programmes are excluded from this package.

- **Ownership:** Private, self-financing
- **Legal category:** Deemed-to-be university

## 3. Admission routes — Official verified

For 2026, first-year admissions use TIET's institute counselling:

- **JEE Main AIR:** branches 1–9 are selected through JEE Main AIR; other listed branches may also accept JEE according to the official programme rule.
- **10+2 PCM:** branches 10–16 may use JEE Main AIR or 10+2 PCM merit.
- **Biomedical Engineering and Biotechnology:** JEE Main AIR, NEET AIR, PCB or PCM merit routes are officially documented.
- **Minimum marks:** 60% in the prescribed 10+2 subject aggregate; 55% for SC/ST.
- **Quota rule:** 50% Punjab and 50% outside Punjab; additional sports and Punjab Government nominee provisions are documented.
- **Direct admission:** may be offered in selected lower-demand branches subject to vacancies.
- **Branch change:** an official regulation describes CGPA/no-backlog requirements, but applicability to the 2026 batch must be reconfirmed.

No management quota was inferred.

## 6. Fees and total cost — Official verified + calculated

Published four-year programme fees, before hostel/mess, fall into three groups:

- **High group:** INR 26.00 lakh programme fee; INR 26.30 lakh including admission
- **Middle group:** INR 23.584 lakh programme fee; INR 23.884 lakh including admission
- **Other programmes:** INR 19.888 lakh programme fee; INR 20.188 lakh including admission

Patiala hostel room charges and tentative mess charges produce current-rate annual residential examples from approximately **INR 1.23 lakh to INR 2.108 lakh**.

### Calculated four-year institutional estimate

Using current rates without inflation:

- **Lower estimate:** approximately INR 25.108 lakh
- **Upper estimate:** approximately INR 34.732 lakh

These are **calculated estimates**, not official totals. Hostel rates can change, mess charges depend on consumption and the official PDF contains a 2025-26/2026-27 session-label conflict.

The fee policy also publishes 20% or 30% tuition waivers for selected branches based on PCM marks, with annual AGPA 8.5 continuation requirements.

## 7. Scholarships — Official verified

`scholarships.csv` contains 14 structured schemes, including:

- Merit Scholarship I: tuition and development fee waiver for the first 50 qualifying JEE AIR students with >95% marks.
- Merit Scholarship II: full tuition waiver for the next 60 with >90%.
- Merit Scholarship III: half tuition for approximately the first 10% in each discipline with >85%.
- PCB merit awards for BME/BT.
- 25–50% lateral-entry tuition scholarships.
- Merit-cum-means tuition/development waivers for family income up to INR 10 lakh.
- Donor scholarships of INR 50,000 per year in specified cases.

Scholarships are not guaranteed and are generally awarded for one academic year at a time. Renewal depends on CGPA/AGPA, backlog status and annual merit.

## 15. Parent Mode summary — Perspective summary

TIET offers a large established residential campus, strong official overall placement medians and substantial scholarship opportunities. The key concerns are high total cost, annual scholarship renewal, rapidly expanded intake, incomplete branch-level placement transparency, strict hostel rules and the missing 2023 cutoff series.

## 16. Student Mode summary — Perspective summary

TIET appears best suited to self-driven students who will use societies, projects and internships while maintaining CGPA. Branch fit matters greatly. Students targeting software from a core branch should verify eligibility history and build both domain and software skills rather than assuming universal access.

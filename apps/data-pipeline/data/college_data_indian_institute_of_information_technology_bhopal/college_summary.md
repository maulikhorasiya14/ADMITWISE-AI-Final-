## 1. Verified college overview — Official verified
IIIT Bhopal is a public-private-partnership Institute of National Importance established in 2017. It currently operates from MANIT Bhopal. JoSAA institute code: **323**. A proposed **60.98-acre** permanent campus is documented at Fatehpur Dogra but is not treated as operational.

## 2. Accreditation and ranking — Official verified / unavailable
Institute of National Importance and autonomous status are verified. Current NAAC, programme-wise NBA and AICTE identifiers were not conclusively verified. No current numeric NIRF Engineering rank was verified.

## 3. Admission routes — Official verified
B.Tech admission is through JEE Main and JoSAA, followed by CSAB Special for vacancies. All regular seats are All India quota. The 2026 matrix has no female-supernumerary seats. Branch change is permitted after first year subject to CGPA, vacancy and programme-strength rules.

## 4. Branches and intake — Official verified
The 2026 matrix contains **9 programmes and 1,188 seats**: CSE 288; IT 180; ECE 180; and 90 each in CSE-AI, CSE-DS, CSE-Cyber Security, CSE-Cyber Physical Systems, Mathematics and Computing, and Physics and Computational Engineering.

## 6. Fees and total cost — Institution-published / calculated
The official 2025-26 B.Tech schedule charges **INR 226,800 tuition per year**. The published institutional total is INR 283,374 in Year I and INR 263,592 in each of Years II-IV. First-year boys hostel options range from INR 57,600 to INR 64,800, with INR 15,000 bus fee, INR 6,000 refundable caution and INR 41,000 pure-vegetarian or INR 43,000 veg/non-veg mess charges.

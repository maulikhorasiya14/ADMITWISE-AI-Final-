# Excluded Sources and Claims

Aggregator sites such as Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh, coaching websites, copied tables and AI-generated summaries were excluded from verified factual data. Anonymous one-line allegations, private-group content, unsupported package claims and claims about faculty or individuals were excluded. Public Reddit and YouTube material is retained only as labelled qualitative evidence. No official 2026 cutoff, guaranteed scholarship, current recruiter visit, completed permanent campus or four-year hostel cost was invented.

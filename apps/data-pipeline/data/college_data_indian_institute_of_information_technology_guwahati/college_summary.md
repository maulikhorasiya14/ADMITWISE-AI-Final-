## 1. Verified college overview — Official verified
IIIT Guwahati is a public-private-partnership Institute of National Importance established in 2013 under the IIIT-PPP framework. It moved to its permanent 67-acre Bongora campus in July 2018. JoSAA institute code: **303**.

## 2. Accreditation and ranking — Official verified / unavailable
The statutory INI and autonomous status are verified. Separate current AICTE, UGC, NAAC and programme-wise NBA records were not conclusively verified. NIRF Engineering bands were **151-200 in 2023**, **151-200 in 2024**, and **201-300 in 2025**.

## 3. Admission routes — Official verified
B.Tech admission is through JEE Main and JoSAA, followed by CSAB Special rounds for vacancies. The supplied data shows All India quota. No home-state, management or institutional quota has been inferred.

## 4. Branches and intake — Official verified
The 2026 matrix contains three B.Tech programmes: CSE (138 regular + 27 female-supernumerary), ECE (75 + 14), and ECAI (64 + 16). Total: **277 regular seats + 57 female-supernumerary = 334 seats**.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — Official verified
The package contains **479 validated rows**: 427 JoSAA rows for 2023-2025 and 52 CSAB Special 2025 rows (Round 1: 22, Round 2: 17, Round 3: 13). Four JoSAA combinations with both ranks blank were excluded rather than treated as zero. JoSAA category ranks and CSAB All India CRL remain separate. ECAI has no historical cutoff in the provided 2023-25 dataset.

## 6. Fees and total cost — Official verified / calculated
Admission Batch 2026 tuition is INR 2.95 lakh, 3.15 lakh, 3.35 lakh and 3.55 lakh across the four years, totalling **INR 13 lakh tuition**. At current published 2026 hostel/mess/gymkhana rates, the calculated four-year hosteller estimate is **INR 1,561,000**, including INR 5,000 refundable caution money. This is not an official fixed total because hostel and mess charges may change annually. The profile explicitly states there is no category fee concession.

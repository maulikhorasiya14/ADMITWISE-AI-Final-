# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source types were excluded from verified factual datasets:

- **Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and coaching portals:** not used for official cutoffs, fees, placements, intake or accreditation because the original official source is available or required.
- **Unsourced package graphics, social posts and copied tables:** excluded when the original placement report, JoSAA/CSAB document or NIRF submission could not be traced.
- **Anonymous rumours and one-line accusations:** excluded from campus-reality summaries.
- **Private groups, inaccessible posts and authentication-gated material:** not accessed or bypassed.
- **Search-engine/AI summaries:** not used as evidence.
- **Highest-package claims without a dated official placement report:** excluded.
- **Student claims about official rules, placement percentages or fees:** not used as verified facts.

Qualitative public sources retained in `student_experience_sources.csv` are clearly labelled anecdotal and do not enter official datasets.

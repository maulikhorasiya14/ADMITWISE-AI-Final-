# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following sources or claim types were not used for verified factual records:

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar aggregators: discovery only; their cutoff, fee, seat and placement figures were excluded unless traced to an original official source.
- Coaching and college-prediction websites: all predicted 2026 cutoffs and admission probabilities were excluded.
- News reports for official placement figures: not imported when an institute or NIRF source was available.
- Anonymous social-media package claims: excluded from placement CSVs.
- Upvotes, one-line reviews and hearsay: not treated as evidence of college-wide conditions.
- Personal accusations, names of private individuals, and sensitive incidents: excluded from the public dataset.
- Private WhatsApp, Telegram and inaccessible groups: not accessed.
- Undated recruiter logos: not interpreted as proof that a company currently visits.
- Search-engine or AI summaries: not treated as sources.

Student sources are retained only for carefully labelled qualitative synthesis and never for official cutoffs, fees, scholarships, placement numbers, accreditation or seat intake.

## 1. Verified college overview — official verified
Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Nagpur is a PPP-model Indian Institute of Information Technology and an Institute of National Importance. Its permanent campus is at Waranga near Butibori in Nagpur district. JoSAA institute code: **320**.

## 2. Accreditation — official verified / unavailable
Institute of National Importance status and NIRF institution ID **IR-E-U-0841** are verified. Current AICTE identifier/applicability, UGC category, NAAC status, programme-wise NBA validity and exact 2025 NIRF Engineering rank remain unverified and are null.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified
B.Tech admission is through JEE Main and JoSAA, with CSAB Special rounds when seats remain. The 2026 matrix is All India quota; no home-state quota or female-supernumerary seats are shown. Branch change is not permitted after admission.

## 4. Branches and intake — official verified
Six four-year B.Tech programmes total **648 seats**: CSE 216, CSE AI & ML 72, CSE Data Science & Analytics 72, CSE HCI & Gaming Technology 72, ECE 144 and ECE IoT 72.

## 6. Fees and total cost — official verified / calculated
The official 2026-27 first-year academic total is **₹246,300**, excluding hostel and mess. A calculated four-year academic estimate of **₹931,200** assumes the current annual fee remains unchanged and excludes hostel, mess, fee increases and personal expenses.

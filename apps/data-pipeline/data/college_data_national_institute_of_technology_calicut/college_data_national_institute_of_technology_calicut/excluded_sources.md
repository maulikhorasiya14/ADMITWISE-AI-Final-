# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source types were intentionally excluded from the verified factual dataset:

- **Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and College Pravesh:** third-party aggregators; cutoff, fee and placement figures were not imported.
- **Coaching and admission-prediction websites:** predictions and copied tables were not treated as official 2026 evidence.
- **Search-engine or AI-generated summaries:** not used as source records.
- **Unsourced “average/highest package” posts:** excluded unless the same claim appeared on an official NITC/NIRF source.
- **Reddit, YouTube and forum numeric claims:** excluded from cutoffs, fees, rankings, scholarship benefits and placement statistics. These sources are used only for qualitative student-experience themes.
- **Private groups, login-only pages and inaccessible social-media content:** not accessed or bypassed.
- **Rumours about ragging, mess quality, faculty or branch placements:** not repeated as facts; only cautious aggregate themes with source limitations are retained.
- **Personal accusations or named private individuals:** excluded.

The user-provided merged cutoff CSV was retained because the user has identified it as the original verified official-source dataset; its 2024 quota-label anomaly is explicitly flagged rather than silently corrected.

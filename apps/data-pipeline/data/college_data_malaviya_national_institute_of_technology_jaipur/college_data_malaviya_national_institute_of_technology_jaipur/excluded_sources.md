# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source categories were not used for verified factual records:

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and College Pravesh: excluded from verified cutoffs, fees, placements, accreditation and intake because they are third-party aggregators.
- Coaching and predictor websites: excluded from official cutoff records and 2026 predictions.
- Unsourced social-media graphics and copied placement tables: excluded unless the original official MNIT report was available.
- Anonymous one-line rumours and personal accusations: excluded from campus-reality summaries.
- Private groups, login-gated material and inaccessible student communities: not accessed.
- YouTube or Reddit numerical claims about fees, cutoffs or packages: not imported as official data.
- Claims that a recruiter offers a particular role/package to a branch: excluded unless published in an official hiring or placement document.

Student-experience sources retained in `student_experience_sources.csv` are used only for clearly labelled qualitative themes and limitations.

# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following sources or claim types were excluded from verified factual records:

- **Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho and College Pravesh:** excluded for cutoffs, fees, placement statistics, admissions and accreditation because original official records were available.
- **Coaching and counselling blogs:** excluded from verified cutoff and admission data.
- **Search-engine or AI-generated summaries:** not used as source records.
- **Undated recruiter-logo pages:** not used to claim current company participation. Recruiters in this package are tied to an official 2022-23 report.
- **YouTube package/cutoff claims:** excluded. Videos were used only for observable campus and hostel context.
- **Anonymous Reddit claims about attendance, ragging, food or placements:** retained only as labelled student-reported evidence. They do not establish official policy or institution-wide quality.
- **Online/non-campus IIT Madras BS programme discussions:** excluded from on-campus B.Tech/B.S./Dual Degree facts and summaries unless clearly relevant to shared campus logistics.
- **Rumours and personal accusations:** excluded entirely.
- **Unverified maximum-salary headlines:** excluded unless an official placement report supplied scope and units.

No inaccessible private group, authentication-gated source or paywalled source was bypassed.

# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following source categories were not used as verified factual evidence:

- Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and coaching websites: secondary aggregators; official JoSAA, NIRF and institute sources were available.
- Unsourced package and placement tables: excluded because CTC, base salary, domestic/international scope and denominator could not be verified.
- Search-engine/AI summaries: excluded as source records.
- Anonymous claims about ragging, drugs, individual staff or personal disputes: excluded because they were not verifiable and could cause harm.
- One-line hostel or mess reviews: not generalized; only selected detailed discussions were retained as limited anecdotal evidence.
- Private groups, sign-in-only pages and inaccessible sources: not accessed or bypassed.
- Historical dual-degree programme names were retained only in cutoff history and were not presented as current 2026 entry programmes.

## 1. Verified college overview — official verified

IIT Kharagpur is a Central Government statutory Indian Institute of Technology and Institute of National Importance, founded in 1951. JoSAA institute code: **106**. The package isolates the main Kharagpur campus and does not mix Kolkata or Bhubaneswar extension-centre records.

## 2. Accreditation and rankings — official verified / unavailable

NIRF Engineering ranks: **5 (2025, score 78.69)**, **5 (2024, score 76.88)** and **6 (2023, score 73.76)**. AICTE, NAAC and programme-level NBA records were not independently verified and remain null rather than inferred.

## 3. Admission routes — official verified

UG entry is through JEE Advanced and JoSAA after satisfying the applicable JEE Main/Class XII eligibility. The quota is All India. Category/PwD reservations and female-supernumerary pools apply. Branch change was abolished from 2024-25. Double Major, Minor/Micro-Specialization, Semester Away and later Dual-Degree options are documented.

## 4. Branches and intake — official verified

The 2026 matrix contains **24 programmes**, **1,749 regular seats**, **200 female-supernumerary seats**, and **1,949 total seats including supernumerary seats**. See `branches.csv` and `college_profile.json`.

## 5. Historical cutoffs — official verified

The original verified cutoff dataset provides **4,794 valid JoSAA records** after excluding **21 blank/no-allotment placeholders**:

- 2023: 2,145
- 2024: 1,179
- 2025: 1,470

All available rounds, programmes, categories, PwD pools and gender pools were retained. No historical value is labelled as an official 2026 cutoff. IITs do not participate in CSAB Special rounds.

## 6. Fees and total cost — institution-published / calculated

The JoSAA 2026-27 table lists tuition of INR 100,000 per semester for the full-tuition category, INR 33,333 for the INR 1-5 lakh family-income band, and nil tuition for SC/ST/PwD and the below-INR-1-lakh income band. Other one-time and semester charges are itemised. The calculated unchanged-rate four-year totals are:

- Full tuition: **INR 1,209,948**
- One-third tuition: **INR 676,612**
- Tuition-exempt: **INR 409,948**

These are calculations, not official fixed totals. The source labels the fee table tentative.

## 7. Scholarships — official/institution-published, eligibility not guaranteed

Ten structured records cover MCM, free tuitionship, Learn-Earn-Return, endowments, conference assistance, statutory tuition exemption/remission and NSP schemes. Current amounts, deadlines and notified-institution matching require verification.

## 8. Placement statistics — institution-published and NIRF-reported

The package contains 20 placement/outcome records across 2022-23, 2023-24 and 2024-25. CDC registration-based data and NIRF graduating-cohort data remain separate. Major official figures include:

- 2022-23 overall CDC: 1,615 placed/accepted from 2,256 registered; 1,742 offers; highest package INR 2.68 crore.
- 2023-24 overall CDC: 1,647 placed/accepted from 2,667 registered; 1,731 offers; 36 international offers.
- 2024-25 overall CDC: 1,735 placed/accepted from 3,090 registered; 1,811 offers; 25 international offers; highest package INR 2.14 crore.

These are CTC/placement-report figures, not guaranteed salary. Scope conflicts are preserved in `data_conflicts.csv`.

## 9. Recruiters — institution-published

Roy Technology Hospital, 24-hour ambulance and SETU counselling. A separate HMC page states 20 halls plus two family accommodations; this is preserved as a conflict.

## 12. Clubs and student activities — institution-associated

## 13. Location and travel — official / map-derived

The campus is approximately 5 km/10 minutes from Kharagpur Junction and roughly 120 km west of Kolkata. Airport and map distances are approximate. The main campus is self-contained but relatively removed from a metro technology ecosystem.

## 14. Student-reported campus reality — anecdotal/conflicting

Public evidence supports a broad society/hall culture and shows hall-room examples, but room, mess and senior-interaction experiences vary. Mess complaints recur, while some accounts are positive. No numeric campus rating is produced.

## 15. Parent Mode summary

The institution has strong verified admissions, programme, seat and historical-cutoff evidence. Confirm the tentative final fee demand, hall allotment, scholarship notice and latest placement report before making a financial decision.

## 16. Student Mode summary

IIT Kharagpur offers broad academics and a large technical/cultural ecosystem. Branch choice should be made carefully because branch change is unavailable. Hall/mess experience varies and city exposure is less immediate than at metro campuses.

## 17. Decision Risk Analysis

Key risks: branch rigidity, tentative fee revision, placement-scope confusion, hall/mess variability, location isolation and scholarship uncertainty.

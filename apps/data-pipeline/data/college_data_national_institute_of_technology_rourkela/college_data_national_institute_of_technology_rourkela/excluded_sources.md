# Excluded Sources and Claims

The following were excluded from verified factual fields:

- **Shiksha, Careers360, Collegedunia, CollegeDekho, College Pravesh and similar aggregators:** figures were not used because original official sources were available or values could not be traced.
- **Coaching and choice-filling websites:** not used for cutoffs, intake or admission rules.
- **Search-engine and AI summaries:** used only to locate original sources, never as evidence.
- **Reddit, YouTube and GeeksforGeeks accounts:** retained only for qualitative, explicitly student-reported campus themes.
- **Unofficial placement spreadsheets, screenshots and package rumours:** excluded because scope and units were not verifiable.
- **Current scholarship amounts inferred from old circulars:** historical amounts are labelled historical and not presented as guaranteed current benefits.
- **Unverified campus coordinates, travel time, hospital distance and city-centre distance:** left null rather than guessed.
- **Absolute safety claims, personal accusations and public usernames:** excluded.
- **Repeated copies of the April 2026 mess/water complaint:** treated as one underlying incident rather than independent corroboration.

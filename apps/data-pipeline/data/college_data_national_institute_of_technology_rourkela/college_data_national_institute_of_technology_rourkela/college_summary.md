## 1. Verified college overview — official verified

National Institute of Technology, Rourkela (NITR) was established in 1961 as Regional Engineering College, Rourkela and became NIT Rourkela after receiving autonomy in 2002. It is a centrally funded National Institute of Technology and an Institute of National Importance. Its JoSAA institute code is **223**. [SRC001, SRC007]

## 2. Accreditation and ranking — official verified / unavailable

NIRF Engineering ranks are **13 in 2025 (score 66.62), 19 in 2024 (63.38), and 16 in 2023 (62.79)**. The official NBA data page lists ten UG programme data downloads, but current validity periods were not confirmed. Current NAAC status is left unavailable rather than guessed. [SRC008-SRC010, SRC014]

## 3. Admission routes — official verified

Current UG allocation is through JEE Main and JoSAA, with CSAB Special rounds for notified vacancies. The 2026 profile uses Odisha and Other Than Odisha quota pools and states **NO BRANCH CHANGE**. Exact 2026 board eligibility must be checked in current JEE Main/JoSAA rules. [SRC001]
